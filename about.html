Biography:

Onika Tanya Maraj-Petty, known professionally as Nicki Minaj, is a Trinidadian-American rapper, singer, songwriter, actress and model.

Background:

Born: December 8, 1982 (age 37 years), Saint James, Port of Spain, Trinidad and Tobago Full name: Onika Tanya Maraj Spouse: Kenneth Petty (m. 2019).

Career:

Nicki Minaj has released 4 studio albums, 3 compilation albums, 3 mixtapes, 100 and 15 singles (including 77 as a featured artist), and 15 promotional singles.

Albums:

Queen, The Pinkprint, Pink Friday: Roman, Reloaded, Pink Friday.

Features:

5 Star, 2012, All Eyes on You, All I Do Is Win, Anybody, Bad to You, Bad for You, Ball for Me, Bang Bang.

Education:

Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School of Music & Art and Performing Arts.
